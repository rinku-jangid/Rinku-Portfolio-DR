About Piramal Critical Care

Piramal Critical Care (PCC), a business division of Piramal Pharma Limited (PPL), a subsidiary of Piramal Enterprises Limited, is the third largest producer of inhaled anesthetics and a global player in hospital generics. ‘Saving and improving patients’ lives’ is much more than a purpose statement to us.

We are motivated by our vision of commitment to delivering critical care solutions for patients and healthcare providers across the globe, while achieving sustainable and profitable growth for all the stakeholders.
